## The Firm

Valnee Solutions is a software agency that works with founders at two specific stages: before a product exists, and after a product exists but is causing problems.

We take on a small number of clients each quarter and assign senior practitioners to every engagement. The work we deliver is documented, owned by the client from day one, and built to be handed off cleanly. The people who do the work are the people you speak to throughout the engagement.

## What Valnee Is Not

- **Not a dev shop with a bench.** No junior developers receiving task assignments from a project manager who doesn't write code.
- **Not an offshore delivery center.** We don't compete on rate arbitrage. The value is in quality, documentation, and accountability.
- **Not a design agency that builds on the side.** Software engineering is the primary discipline here. Design, copywriting, and brand work are outside our scope.
- **Not a product studio taking equity.** Valnee is paid for its work at market rates. We don't take equity positions in client companies.
- **Not a managed services provider.** We build and hand off. Ongoing maintenance after delivery is available in limited form, but the default is that the client owns and operates what was built.

## How We Operate

**Scope in writing, always.**
Before any code is written, the scope is documented and agreed on in writing. Changes are handled through a written change request with time and cost implications noted before the change is accepted.

**You own the code from day one.**
The repository lives in your version control account from the first commit. If the engagement ends for any reason, you have full access to everything built up to that point.

**Documentation is a deliverable, not a footnote.**
Every engagement ends with a handoff package: architecture documentation, a deployment runbook, and a recorded walkthrough of the delivered system. The standard: a competent developer who's never seen the codebase should be able to understand, deploy, and extend it using only the documentation provided.

**Decisions are explained, not just made.**
Every major technical decision is recorded with the alternatives considered and the reasoning behind the choice. For non-technical founders, these are written in plain language.

**No surprises on timeline or cost.**
If something happens that will affect the timeline or cost, we communicate it immediately in writing — not at the end of the cycle, not after the delay is unavoidable, at the point of discovery.

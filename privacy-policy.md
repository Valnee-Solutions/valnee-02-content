Valnee Solutions ("we", "us", "our") operates the Valnee Solutions website. This page describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We keep this simple because we keep our data practices simple.

---

## What We Collect

**Information you provide directly.**
When you submit a project brief or contact us by email, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, company or project name, project description, and any other details you choose to share. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and assess whether your project is a fit for our services.

**Basic website analytics.**
We may collect standard website analytics data — pages visited, time on page, referring URLs, and general geographic region (country level). This data is aggregated and not linked to individual identities. It helps us understand which content is useful to visitors.

**Email communication.**
When you email us directly at hello@valnee.com, we store that correspondence for the purpose of responding to you and maintaining a record of our communication.

---

## How We Use Your Information

- To respond to project inquiries and assess fit for our services.
- To schedule and conduct scoping calls and send the resulting documentation.
- To send project-related communication during active engagements.
- To improve the content and structure of this website based on aggregated usage data.

We do not use your information for advertising, cold outreach, or automated marketing sequences.

---

## What We Don't Do

- We do not sell your information to third parties.
- We do not share your information with third parties except where required to deliver our services (for example, scheduling tools or document platforms used in the course of an engagement) or where required by law.
- We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

---

## Data Retention

We retain project inquiry information for as long as the inquiry is active plus a reasonable period for follow-up. We retain communication records from active client engagements for a minimum of three years for legal and operational purposes.

If you would like your information removed from our records, email us at hello@valnee.com and we will action your request within 10 business days, subject to any legal retention obligations.

---

## Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information you provide. Communication with us over email is subject to standard email security practices. If you are sharing sensitive information (credentials, financial data, personally identifiable data about your users), we recommend discussing a secure transfer method before sharing.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We do not operate a subscriber list for policy updates — if this policy matters to your engagement with us, check this page periodically.

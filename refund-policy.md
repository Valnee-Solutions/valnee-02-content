Valnee Solutions is built on predictable delivery and transparent financial terms. This policy is designed to eliminate the financial ambiguity that non-technical founders commonly face when starting a software project.

## The On-Time Delivery Guarantee

The most important aspect of an MVP launch is predictability. This guarantee covers our commitment against internal delays.

### Guarantee Eligibility

This guarantee applies to all projects contracted under a defined fixed-scope engagement where the project timeline and launch date are explicitly agreed upon in a signed Scope of Work (SOW).

### What the Guarantee Covers

If Valnee Solutions misses the fixed launch date due to internal delays, resource issues, or errors in our development process:

- We will pause all billing after the agreed-upon launch date.
- We will continue development at no additional cost until the MVP is fully completed, deployed, and delivered according to the final SOW.

You receive the full product, even if we are late, without incurring additional fees.

### Exceptions

The On-Time Delivery Guarantee protects you from our failures. It does not apply to delays caused by the client or external factors:

**Client-requested scope changes:** Any material changes, additions, or modifications to the initial SOW requested by the client will require a written Change Order, which will produce a revised timeline and nullify the original launch date guarantee.

**Client delays:** Delays resulting from the client's failure to provide required content, access credentials, feedback, or approvals within the timelines specified in the SOW.

**Force majeure:** Delays caused by circumstances outside either party's reasonable control, including acts of God, war, terrorism, government action, or infrastructure failures affecting third-party services.

## Refund Policy for Project Cancellation

### Stage 1: Blueprint and Strategy Phase

The Blueprint and Strategy Phase is a standalone, paid service that delivers a fixed-price, fixed-scope technical roadmap.

If the client cancels before development (Stage 2) begins:

- The fee paid for the Blueprint and Strategy Phase is **non-refundable**. The strategic work and documentation have been delivered.
- Any payments made in advance for future development stages (Stage 2 or 3) will be **fully refunded within 10 business days**.

### Stage 2: Build Phase

The Build Phase is billed based on progress milestones.

If the client cancels during the Build Phase:

- Refunds are processed only for services not yet rendered. We will calculate all completed work based on the project milestones and the agreed-upon hourly rate or milestone completion percentage.
- The remaining unused portion of any advance payment will be **refunded within 15 business days**.
- Upon cancellation, all completed code and intellectual property for which the client has paid will be immediately transferred and delivered to the client.

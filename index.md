## How We Work

Every engagement follows the same five steps.

**1. Intake and Scoping** — A structured 45-minute call. Written scope document within 5 days. No work starts without your sign-off.

**2. Technology Selection or Audit** — For new builds, we document every stack decision. For existing codebases, we audit before we recommend anything.

**3. Delivery in 2-Week Cycles** — You review working, deployed software at the end of every cycle — not a status report, not a slide deck.

**4. Documentation and Handoff** — Architecture docs, deployment runbook, walkthrough video. Written throughout the engagement, not compressed into the last week.

**5. Post-Delivery Review** — Two weeks after delivery, a structured call to close out any gaps and confirm everything meets the agreed scope.

## Common Questions

**How much does a project cost?**
Most MVP builds run $15,000–$45,000. Codebase rescue engagements run $8,000–$25,000. Architecture work is typically $3,000–$8,000 as a fixed fee. All pricing is confirmed in a written scope document before work begins.

**How long does a project take?**
MVP builds: 8–12 weeks from scoping to deployed product. Codebase rescue: 4–8 weeks. Integration work: 2–4 weeks. Timelines are fixed in the scope document — we're accountable to them.

**Do I need to be technical?**
No. Several clients have no technical background. We adapt communication accordingly — plain-language summaries, not pull request reviews.

**Who owns the code?**
You do. The repository is in your account from day one. No proprietary tooling, no vendor lock-in.

**What if I have a limited budget?**
Valnee is not the cheapest option. If your budget is under $10,000, a standard engagement is probably not the right fit. The Developer Store offers hourly access without the overhead of a full project.

**What if I'm not satisfied?**
Every delivery cycle ends with a client review. If the delivered work doesn't match the agreed scope, we fix it. That's what the scope document and review process exist for.

**Can you maintain the software after delivery?**
Post-delivery support is limited to the two-week review period by default. Ongoing support is available through the Developer Store or a retainer arrangement. See [services](services.md) for details.

**How do I get started?**
[Submit a project brief](waitlist.md). We review every submission within two business days. If it's not a fit, we'll tell you directly and suggest a better path.

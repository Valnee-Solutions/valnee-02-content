## Project Pluto

**Client type:** Logistics SaaS, Series A stage, 12-person team

**Problem:** Order processing failures in a two-year-old Rails application had grown from
an occasional inconvenience to a material business risk. The platform was processing
shipping orders at a 94 percent success rate — acceptable at early volume and damaging at
the volume the company had reached.

**What Valnee did:** Conducted a full codebase audit, identified three systemic failure
points in the order processing pipeline, and rebuilt the pipeline without altering the
customer-facing front end or the surrounding application logic. Each change was deployed
behind a feature flag and tested against production traffic patterns before full rollout.

**Result:** Order processing reliability reached 99.7 percent within six weeks of the
rebuilt pipeline going into production. Deployment time for the order pipeline specifically
dropped from a manual 90-minute process to an automated 8-minute deployment.

## Layers Landing Page

**Client type:** Solo founder, B2B productivity SaaS, pre-seed stage

**Problem:** A founder with a validated product concept, no technical background, and a
six-month runway needed a working product built and in the hands of early users within
that window. She had no technical co-founder and no existing code.

**What Valnee did:** Completed a scoping and technology selection engagement in the first
two weeks, documented every technical decision, and built a fully deployed SaaS MVP with
user authentication, a subscription billing integration, and the core product workflow.
The founder reviewed working software at the end of each two-week delivery cycle.

**Result:** The product was delivered in eleven weeks, within the original scope and budget.
The founder launched to her waitlist of 340 subscribers, onboarded 60 paying users in the
first month, and closed a pre-seed round three months after launch.

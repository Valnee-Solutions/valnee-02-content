## Project Pluto

**Client type:** Logistics SaaS, Series A stage, 12-person team

**Problem:** Order processing failures in a two-year-old Rails application had grown from an occasional inconvenience to a material business risk. The platform was processing shipping orders at a 94% success rate — acceptable at early volume, damaging at the volume the company had reached.

**What Valnee did:** Conducted a full codebase audit, identified three systemic failure points in the order processing pipeline, and rebuilt the pipeline without altering the customer-facing front end or surrounding application logic. Each change was deployed behind a feature flag and tested against production traffic before full rollout.

**Result:** Order processing reliability reached 99.7% within six weeks of the rebuilt pipeline going live. Deployment time for the order pipeline dropped from a manual 90-minute process to an automated 8-minute deployment.

## Layers Landing Page

**Client type:** Solo founder, B2B productivity SaaS, pre-seed stage

**Problem:** A founder with a validated product concept, no technical background, and a six-month runway needed a working product built and in the hands of early users within that window. No technical co-founder, no existing code.

**What Valnee did:** Completed scoping and technology selection in the first two weeks, documented every technical decision, and built a fully deployed SaaS MVP with user authentication, subscription billing integration, and the core product workflow. The founder reviewed working software at the end of each two-week delivery cycle.

**Result:** Product delivered in eleven weeks, within the original scope and budget. The founder launched to a waitlist of 340 subscribers, onboarded 60 paying users in the first month, and closed a pre-seed round three months after launch.
